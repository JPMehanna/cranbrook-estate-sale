The Sale

A home full of designer pieces — offered privately.

As we prepare to sell our Northville home, we are offering a private estate sale by appointment only, featuring high-end furnishings, designer pieces, décor, kitchenware, fitness equipment, and household items throughout the home.

The collection includes items from the kitchen, dining room, living room, bedrooms, library/home office, lower-level entertaining areas, and home gym — along with fine china, serving pieces, decorative accessories, and many additional designer-brand items.

There is simply too much to list individually, so serious buyers are invited to schedule a private appointment to walk through the home, view available items in person, ask questions, and discuss pricing.
